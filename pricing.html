Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services

What is included in each pricing tier?

+

Each plan includes core features with increasing capabilities. Starter includes basic templates and community support. Professional adds unlimited projects, priority support, and advanced analytics. Enterprise provides custom solutions with dedicated account management and 24/7 support.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription?

+

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. We'll prorate any charges or credits based on your usage.

What are your billing and payment terms?

+

We offer flexible monthly and annual billing options. Annual plans include a 20% discount. We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise accounts. Invoices are issued automatically upon payment.

Is there a free trial available?

+

Yes, we provide a 14-day free trial for all plans with full feature access. No credit card is required to start. After the trial period, you can choose to subscribe or your account will be archived.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

+

You can cancel your subscription anytime without penalties. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation.

Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?

+

Absolutely. Our Enterprise plan includes custom integrations, white-label options, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements and receive a tailored proposal.
